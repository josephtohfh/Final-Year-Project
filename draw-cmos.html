How To Draw

Enter CMOS circuit equation of 3 to 5 inputs

  • Each input should be represented by letter
  • Letter Z should not be used
  • The operations AND and OR are represented by * and + respectively
  • The operation NOT is represented by '
  • Equation should have at most 2 input inversion i.e. A'
  • Equation should have at most 1 output inversion
  • Output inversion can be added with ( at the start and )' at the end

Examples

A+B*C B*C+D' (I'+E+M')'
(A+B)*C*D' (C'+M)*(O'+S) (D*C*B'*A)'
(A+B)*C*D'+E (B+C)*(D+E+A) (C*(I+G*(L+O')))'
